Coding Discussion Group – University of Manitoba


Coding Discussion Group







I’m pleased to be directing the upcoming Coding Discussion Group for the Linguistics Department at the University of Manitoba, to take place in 2020:

  • Discussions will concern various aspects of the use of code-based software such as R, Praat, and LaTeX in linguistic research
  • Anyone who is interested in participating should email me at sky@onosson.com
  • Once we have a concrete schedule established, it will be posted here and distributed by email through the LING-Listserv mailing list

Schedule

We have tentatively established the upcoming meeting dates as:

  • February: Friday the 7th, 10:30–12;30
  • March: Monday the 2nd, 12:30–2:30
  • April: Monday the 6th, 12:30–2:30

Agenda

For the first meeting on Feb. 7, I have picked the theme of “vowel plotting”. This is a topic we explored as part of one of the workshop meetings in December, but there are many more aspects which we can look at including:

  • organizing/tidying data
  • subsetting (e.g. by phonological context)
  • normalization procedures
  • formant trajectories
  • GAMMs analysis
  • etc.!

I will have some resources prepared for the meeting related to this theme. Also, please don’t hesitate to bring in any questions/problems/topics you would like to discuss, even if they are not connected to the theme.
